who am I?

I am someone who thrives on self-expression through creation. Whether I am writing code, designing visuals, or building digital worlds, I view every project as an opportunity to be authentically myself. For me, creating isn't just a skill — it's a space where technology and my true identity connect.

why did I choose software engineering?

My journey into software engineering stems from a deep passion for creativity. Where others see pure logic, I see an artistic medium. I view coding as a modern brush that allows me to build innovative, visually compelling, and impactful digital experiences.

my future?

Looking ahead, my ultimate goal is to bridge the gap between technology and imagination by pursuing a career in game development or graphic design. I've held a deep, lifelong passion for gaming, and my dream is to build a universe from scratch — using my software engineering foundation as the toolkit and my creative instincts as the guide. I don't just want to play games; I want to create immersive, visually stunning experiences that leave a lasting impact on players, utilizing every bit of technical and artistic knowledge I've gathered.
